# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What products does the company offer?
The company offers a range of digital and physical products designed to improve productivity and customer experience. These include software tools, analytics solutions, and subscription-based services.

### How can I contact customer support?
Customers can contact customer support via email or through the official support portal. Support requests are typically answered within **24–48 hours**.

---

## Orders & Payments

### What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and selected digital wallets. Payment processing is handled securely through trusted payment providers.

### Can I modify my order after placing it?
Orders can be modified before shipment. Once shipped, modifications are not possible.

---

## Shipping & Delivery

### How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes **5–7 business days**. Express shipping options may be available at an additional cost.

### Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, international shipping is available to selected countries. Delivery timelines may vary based on location and customs processing.

---

## Refunds & Returns

### How do I request a refund?
To request a refund, contact customer support with your order number and reason for return. Refunds are processed within **5 business days** after approval.

### Are there any non-refundable items?
Digital products, activated subscriptions, and customized items are non-refundable.

---

## Account & Security

### How is my data protected?
Customer data is encrypted and stored securely. Access is limited to authorized personnel only.

### Can I delete my account?
Yes, customers may request account deletion by contacting customer support. Account data will be permanently deleted within **7 business days**.

---

## Technical Support

### What should I do if a product is defective?
If you receive a defective product, contact customer support within **7 days of delivery**. Replacement or refund options will be provided.

### Do you offer technical training?
Basic usage guides and documentation are provided. Advanced training services may be available on request.
