# Company Policies and Guidelines

## Refund Policy
Our company is committed to providing high-quality products and customer satisfaction. Customers are eligible to request a refund within **30 days of purchase**. To be eligible for a refund, the product must be unused, undamaged, and returned in its original packaging along with the receipt or proof of purchase.

Refund requests are reviewed within **2 business days**, and approved refunds are processed within **5 business days** to the original payment method. Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to a company error or defective product.

## Cancellation Policy
Orders can be cancelled before shipment without penalty. Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled and must instead follow the refund and return process. Digital services or subscriptions cannot be cancelled once activated.

## Privacy Policy
We respect customer privacy and ensure that personal data is handled securely. Customer data is used solely for order processing, communication, and service improvement. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties without explicit consent, except where required by law.

All customer data is stored using industry-standard encryption and security practices.

## Warranty Policy
Products are covered under a **one-year limited warranty** from the date of purchase. This warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover damages caused by misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications.

Customers must contact customer support with proof of purchase to initiate a warranty claim.

## Customer Responsibilities
Customers are responsible for providing accurate shipping and contact information. Any delays or issues caused by incorrect information are not the responsibility of the company.

## Policy Updates
The company reserves the right to update or modify these policies at any time. Updated policies will be published on the official website and apply to all future purchases.
